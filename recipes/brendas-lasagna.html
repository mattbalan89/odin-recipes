Brenda's Lasagna

Good lasagna for family dinner.

Prep time: 30 minutes. Cook time: 30 minutes. Servings: 8. Yield 1 9x13 inch pan.

Ingredients lists

  • lasagna noodles
  • lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • spaghetti sauce
  • garlic clove, minced
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil. add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  2. preheat oven to 350 F. in large skilled over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. in medium bow, combine mazzarella, chedder, and ricotta; stir well. in 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
