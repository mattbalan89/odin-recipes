Matt's Jerky

Beef Jerky

Ingredients

  • beef
  • soy sauce
  • worcestershire sauce
  • brown sugar
  • liquid smoke
  • onion powder
  • chili powder
  • garlic powder
  • cayenne pepper
  • black pepper

Directions

  1. Cut beef against the grain into 1/4 inch strips
  2. Whisk soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, liquid smoke, onion powder, chili powder, garlic powder, cayenne, and pepper together in large bowl. Add beef strips. cover and marinate in fridge; 18 - 24 hours.
  3. Dehydrate at 160F about 8 hours.
